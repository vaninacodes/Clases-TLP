to hell with it

to hell with it is the debut mixtape from English singer, songwriter and record producer PinkPantheress, following her introduction into the music scene with a string of viral singles on the video-sharing social media platform TikTok. The project is released under Parlophone Records. PinkPantheress first teased the release of her debut project in the official music video for the mixtape’s lead single, August 2021’s “Just for me.” The video ended with the teaser date “October 15th.” She released the follow-up single “I must apologise” on October 6, 2021.
